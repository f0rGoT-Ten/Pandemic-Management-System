About Our System

I. E-Doctor: Here, you will find the symptoms of the Virus (Coronavirus), its primary treatment and the necessary steps that should be taken.
II. ICU: Here, you will be able to find the total number of ICU list and its Hospital name, Location, Tele-phone numbers and the vacancy list.
III. Blood Bank: Here, you will be able to find the total number of the blood donor list and their name, Location, Contact numbers and past date of donation.
IV. Medicine Facilities: Here, you will find the Name list of medical stores that provides door to door service, Location (branch) and their Cell numbers.
V. Vaccination: You can get to know about vaccine availability.
VI. Pandemic Updates: It will keep you updated about the number of people affected people, recovery rate and the death rate per day.
